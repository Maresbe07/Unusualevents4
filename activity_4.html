Activity 4

Life experiences

Listen to John's life experiences interview.

Then, check your understanding below.

Checking your comprehension

Read the statements about John's life experiences interview

and say if they are true or false

Question 1

1. John hasn't met a famous person.                            

Feedback

False

The answer is incorrect because he said: Yes, I've met Nicholas Cage. It was about 3 years ago, I was in the shopping mall and there was a big crowd of people, they were all taking photos. I had to wait a long time because many people wanted to take a picture. I finally got a selfie with him. He was very friendly. I'll show it to you.

Question 2

2. John has been on television.

Feedback

True

The answer is correct because John said: Yes, actually, I have. It was last year, I was watching a big football game with my dad. The game was on TV and the camera showed us in the crowd.

Question 3

3. He has broken a bone.                                  

Feedback

True

The answer is correct because he said: My arm. I was on holiday with my parents. I was running down a hill and fell onto some stones.

Question 4

4. John has climbed mountains.

Feedback

True

The answer is correct because he said: Yes, I've climbed several mountains. My favorite was Showdon in Wales, it was warm and sunny when we climbed it, that's why I liked it. The other mountains were in Scotland, and we climbed them in the rain. I want to climb the tallest mountain in England next. I haven't climbed any England mountains.

Question 5

5. He has sent Valentine's Day cards.

Feedback

True

The answer is correct because he said: Yes, I have. I sent one earlier this year and one last year, too.

Question 6

6. He has been to England climbing mountains.                 

Feedback

False

The answer is correct because he said: I want to climb the tallest mountain in England next. I haven't climbed any England mountains.

Question 7

7. John has met Nicholas Cage.                         

Feedback

True

The answer is correct because he said: Yes, I've met Nicholas Cage. It was about 3 years ago, I was in the shopping mall and there was a big crowd of people, they were all taking photos. I had to wait a long time because many people wanted to take a picture. I finally got a selfie with him. He was very friendly. I'll show it to you.

Question 8

8. He has broken a leg.                                       

Feedback

False

This answer is incorrect because he hasn't broken his leg, he has broken his arm. He answered: My arm. I was on holiday with my parents. I was running down a hill and fell onto some stones.

Question 9

9. He has never gone to Scotland.                      

Feedback

False

The answer is correct because he said: The other mountains were in Scotland, and we climbed them in the rain.

Question 10

10. He has climbed 10 mountains.                        

Feedback

False

The answer is correct because he didn't mention a number, he said: Yes, I've climbed several mountains.
