Activity 1

Life experiences

Multi-choice

Question

1. Has Marcy ever traveled abroad?

Answers

A. Yes, Marcy has traveled to London.

B. No, Marcy hasn't traveled abroad.

Feedback

Excellent!

Marcy said: That happened when I was traveling to London.

On the other hand, the answer is grammatically correct because the use of the auxiliary verb "has" agrees with the subject "she" and it is the long answer to answer questions with the present perfect tense. Moreover, the past participle "traveled" is used to form the present perfect tense.

Try again.

Marcy said: That happened when I was traveling to London.

On the other side: The answer is incorrect because this is the long negative form to answer questions with the present perfect tense for a third person.
